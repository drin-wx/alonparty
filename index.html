Alon Partylist Platform & Advocacy

Every great change begins with a single wave. That is what ALON PARTYLIST stands for—a movement rising with purpose, unity, and genuine service. We believe in an inclusive and safe campus free from bullying, a student council rooted in transparency, and a leadership that genuinely listens to every voice. Above all, we champion the well-being and mental health of every student, because true leaders do not seek power; they earn trust by doing what is right for the community. Together with ALON Partylist, let us move forward and create positive change.

  • Zero Tolerance for BullyingWe advocate for a school environment where bullying in all its forms, whether physical, verbal, social, or online is not tolerated. Every student deserves to feel safe, respected, and valued.
  • Respect for Every IndividualWe stand firmly against all forms of abuse, harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and any behavior that violates the dignity and rights of others. Our community should be built on respect, inclusivity, and compassion.
  • Integrity and Transparency in LeadershipWe are committed to honest, transparent, and accountable leadership. We reject dishonesty, deception, misinformation, and actions that undermine the trust of the student body. Every decision and initiative should be guided by truth, fairness, and responsibility.
  • Accountability and ResponsibilityWe believe that every leader must be accountable for their actions and decisions. Leadership is a responsibility that requires fairness, consistency, and dedication to serving the interests of all students.
  • A Culture of Unity and Mutual RespectWe will promote cooperation, open communication, and understanding among students, fostering a school community where everyone has the opportunity to be heard and respected.
